Customer Eligibility

  • Customer must be 18 years of age or older.
  • Member must possess a valid Light Motor Vehicle (Non Transport).
  • Payments are only accepted through credit card, debit card or net banking
  • There is no minimum amount of time that an individual must hold a license before reserving a vehicle
  • Members must have had no alcohol or drug related driving violations in the past.

Our Terms And Conditions

  • There will be no refunds after booking.
  • Minimum duration for booking a car is 6 hours and maximum of 24 hours.
  • Our vehicles are free of fuel charges. We will provide the fuel for your journey.
  • For safety purposes, while driving, wearing seat belts is mandatory.
  • All the paper works of our vehicles are up to date and the vehicles are fit to be taken anywhere across the country.
  • If the customer loses the papers of the vehicle he have to pay for it.
  • In case of traffic, parking violations and over speeding, the customer have to pay all the fines.
  • In case of any accidents, the customer have to pay 50% of the overall demurrage cost.
  • Vehicles must be picked up and returned as per the location. If not, then there will be some fines added up to 500tk.
  • If a vehicle is returned late, a fine of up to 300tk will be added on the total charge.
  • Intentional damage to the vehicle or lose of any parts of the vehicle, or keys, the customer have to pay for it.
  • After placing a booking or subscription, .if the customer do not receive the vehicle or cancels the vehicle at the last moment, some charges will be added to his/her account.
  • In case of any conflicts between the agreements we have all the rights to terminate any kind of memberships.
